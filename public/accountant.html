Tax Security & Compliance FAQs

Answers regarding the FTC Safeguards Rule, IRS Publication 4557, and browser E2EE.

Under the updated FTC Safeguards Rule, financial institutions (including accounting and tax preparation firms) must encrypt all customer information both in transit and at rest. Because FileDrop Pro encrypts documents *locally* inside your client's web browser before transmitting, files travel securely over HTTPS and reside in AWS storage as highly scrambled, encrypted ciphertext. This fulfills and exceeds regulatory encryption requirements.

Our platform is designed specifically with ease-of-use in mind. Elderly and non-technical clients often struggle with traditional portals because they forget passwords, can't remember usernames, or lose reset codes. With FileDrop Pro, clients simply click your direct drop link and drag and drop their files—no account creation, passwords, or onboarding friction whatsoever.
